Why I Built SHIZEN

Over four years ago, I embarked on my Japanese learning journey from scratch. Like many others, I relied heavily on Anki for memorization. While effective, the process often felt mechanical and disconnected from real Japanese content.

As both a language learner and developer, I saw an opportunity with modern AI technology to create something different. SHIZEN was born from the desire to make immersion-based learning more natural and enjoyable.

The app transforms your favorite Japanese content—whether it's YouTube videos, podcasts, or personal recordings—into interactive learning materials. It's the tool I wished I had when I started my journey.
