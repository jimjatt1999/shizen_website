Core Functionality

Powerful features designed to transform your Japanese learning experience

Intelligent Audio Processing

Import Japanese audio from various sources including podcasts, YouTube videos, and personal recordings. Our intelligent engine segments content into natural learning units.

  • Support for multiple audio formats (MP3, M4A, AIFF, WAV)
  • Smart segmentation based on natural speech patterns
  • Volume normalization and noise reduction
  • Audio waveform visualization for precise segment editing

Text Analysis & Language Tools

Analyze Japanese language structure with comprehensive tools. Break down complex sentences, understand grammar, and build vocabulary.

  • Japanese text parsing and grammar analysis
  • Kanji information with readings and common compounds
  • Word frequency statistics to identify important vocabulary
  • Text highlighting for easier comprehension

Personalized SRS Algorithm

Our advanced spaced repetition system adapts to your learning style and retention patterns. Study smarter, not harder, with precisely timed reviews that optimize memory consolidation.

  • Personalized intervals based on performance
  • Multiple difficulty levels for honest self-assessment
  • Visual learning progress indicators
  • Smart scheduling to prevent review pile-ups

Filtering & Search
