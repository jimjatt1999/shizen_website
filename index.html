Core Functionality

Powerful features designed to transform your Japanese learning experience

Intelligent Audio Processing

Import Japanese audio from various sources including podcasts, YouTube videos, and personal recordings. Our intelligent engine segments content into natural learning units.

  • Support for multiple audio formats (MP3, M4A, AIFF, WAV)
  • Smart segmentation based on natural speech patterns
  • Volume normalization and noise reduction
  • Audio waveform visualization for precise segment editing

Text Analysis & Language Tools

Analyze Japanese language structure with comprehensive tools. Break down complex sentences, understand grammar, and build vocabulary.

  • Japanese text parsing and grammar analysis
  • Kanji information with readings and common compounds
  • Word frequency statistics to identify important vocabulary
  • Text highlighting for easier comprehension

Personalized SRS Algorithm

Our advanced spaced repetition system adapts to your learning style and retention patterns. Study smarter, not harder, with precisely timed reviews that optimize memory consolidation.

  • Personalized intervals based on performance
  • Multiple difficulty levels for honest self-assessment
  • Visual learning progress indicators
  • Smart scheduling to prevent review pile-ups

Learning Tools

Interactive exercises and games designed to enhance comprehension and retention

Missing Word Challenge

Enhance comprehension by identifying strategically blurred words within a transcript.

Word Arrangement Exercise

Improve syntax understanding by reconstructing Japanese sentences from scrambled words.

Fill in the Blank

Test your vocabulary knowledge by filling in missing words based on context and audio cues.

Continuous Playback

Review multiple audio segments in sequence for extended practice sessions.

Study Notes

Add personalized notes and mnemonics to each segment to create context.

Text-to-Speech Integration

Generate synthetic audio for Japanese text using the macOS Kyoko voice.

Content Management

Powerful organization tools to manage your learning materials

Source Organization

Group segments by source material for contextual learning. Keep episodes, lectures, or conversations organized for a coherent learning experience.

Filtering & Search

Easily find specific content with powerful search and filtering tools. Filter by difficulty, tags, dates, or search by transcript content.

Backup & Import

Comprehensive backup system to preserve your progress and study materials. Export and import functionality for transferring between devices.

Metadata Management

Track detailed information about each audio segment including source, date added, review history, and difficulty ratings.
